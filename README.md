# Museum Project: Signal & Script
**Topic:** History of Communication Technology
**Status:** Midterm Review (Spring 2026)

## 1. Orchestration Process
I utilized a **Spec-Driven Development** workflow:
- **Spec Phase:** Identified the **Sage** archetype and **Swiss Design** style as the foundation.
- **Review Phase:** Performed an AI-assisted review as a **Smithsonian Curator** to ensure narrative flow and educational value.
- **Implementation:** Selected Python (Streamlit) for its ability to handle rapid prototyping of complex layout grids.

## 2. Design Frameworks
- **Design Style:** **Swiss (International Typographic Style)**. Characterized by bold primary colors (Red/Yellow) and heavy black grids to provide a high-authority museum feel.
- **Cialdini Principle:** **Authority**. By using verified historical timelines and curated artifact descriptions, the site encourages deep user engagement.
- **Brand Archetype:** **The Sage**. The tone of the site is enlightening and wise, aimed at educating the visitor on the human impact of communication tech.
